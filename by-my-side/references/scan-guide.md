# WeChat Scan Guide

The bridge uses personal WeChat iLink QR login.

Tell the user:

1. Keep the setup command running.
2. Use WeChat to scan the printed QR code or open the printed QR URL.
3. Confirm login in WeChat.
4. Wait for `WeChat iLink account configured.`
5. Send one message to the bridge from WeChat so the bridge receives a reply context.
6. Send `/status` and `/help` to verify the bridge.

Do not say the agent can scan or approve login for them. The user must do that part.
